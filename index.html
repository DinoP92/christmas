Timeline of Christmas

Christmas originated with ancient winter solstice festivals, blending Roman, Norse, and Christian traditions. In the 4th century, December 25 became the established date for celebrating the birth of Jesus. Through the centuries, Christmas evolved, influenced by the Renaissance, Victorian era, and modern global customs, uniting diverse cultures in festive celebrations.

Evidence-based Information

Evidence-based information about Christmas draws from historical records, religious texts, and cultural traditions. Reliable sources substantiate the holiday's origins, including the birth of Jesus. Historical evidence supports the evolution of Christmas customs, revealing the amalgamation of diverse cultural influences into the globally celebrated festive occasion.
